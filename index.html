Welcome to DMI

Dream Ministry International is a place where people can meet Jesus, engage in life-giving community, and everyone is welcome. We believe in creating a space where people can have authentic encounters with Christ, discover their gifts and use them for God's glory.

David Lah

David Lah was called into the Ministry at the age of Nineteen and in 2006 served as Pastor to a local Myanmar (Burmese) Congregation in Toronto, Canada. In 2013, David Lah led the first Global Myanmar Youth Conference (GMYC), a Bi-annual Youth Conference held in Chiang Mai impacting many lives including those of Myanmar Celebrities. In 2014, David Lah founded the D.R.E.A.M Ministries International a fresh and youthful voice proclaiming the Gospel with Spiritual Revival and Breakthroughs to all the Myanmar (Burmese) locally and globally effectively harvesting multitudes of young souls into God's Kingdom."

Mission

Bringing Myanmar Youths from across countries and denominations together in: Wholeness of the Holy Spirit, Communion of the Son Jesus Christ and Reverence towards God the Father. Workshops dedicated to equip different Ministries such as; Evangelism, Praise & Worship, Christian Living, Pastoral Ministries Baptism of the Holy Spirit coupled with Sermons and Teachings which are Christ-centered, Holy Spirit led and revealing the Will of God the Father.

Vision

"The least of you will become a thousand, the smallest a mighty nation, I am the Lord; in its time I will do this swiftly" Isaiah 60:22 NIV We believe that in His time, God will raise individuals mightily for His Kingdom and Will on Earth as it is in Heaven.

Core Beliefs

Jesus Christ came to reconcile us with God. He lived a life without sin and willingly died on the cross to pay the penalty for our transgressions. God raised him from the dead and now, by grace, offers as a free gift eternal life to all who follow Christ, by faith, as their Lord and Savior. That is why salvation can be found in Christ alone. Salvation
The Church is meant to be the visible body of Christ, sent into the world to glorify God and proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ The Church
